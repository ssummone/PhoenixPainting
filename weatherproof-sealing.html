Weatherproof House Sealing in Glasgow

Stop Damp & Protect Your Masonry

Water ingress is the #1 enemy of Scottish homes. Our clear, breathable weatherproof sealing protects your brick and stone from freeze-thaw damage, soaking, and organic growth without changing its appearance.

Defense Against Scottish Weather

Deep Penetration

Our sealant absorbs up to 17mm into the masonry, creating an invisible barrier against moisture.

Improve Heat Retention

Dry walls are warm walls. By keeping moisture out, we enable your masonry to retain heat more effectively.

Protect Structure

Prevent "spalling" (crumbling brick face) caused by cycles of freezing and thawing water.

10-Year Guarantee

Our premium sealing treatments are guaranteed to perform for a decade.

Pro-Perla & Facade Cream

We use advanced nano-technology sealants:

  • Super Hydrophobic – Water beads up and rolls off
  • Invisible Finish – Retains the natural look of stone/brick
  • Self-Cleaning – Rain washes away dirt and pollution

Our Process

Thorough preparation is key to effective sealing:

  1. Biocide Wash – Killing all moss, algae, and spores
  2. Steam Cleaning – Deep cleaning the masonry pores
  3. Repointing – Fixing loose mortar (if required)
  4. Sealing – Flooding the wall with sealant for max absorption
