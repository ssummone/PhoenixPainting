Silicone Rendering Glasgow

The Ultimate "No-Paint" Exterior Solution

Experience the power of hydrophobic silicone technology. Silicone Rendering repels water while allowing your home to breathe, providing a self-cleaning, low-maintenance finish that lasts for decades. Serving Glasgow (G12, G41) and beyond.

Why Choose Silicone Rendering?

Hydrophobic Technology

Water-repellent surface that ensures your walls stay dry and clean, even in Scottish weather.

Never Paint Again

The colour is integrated into the render itself, meaning no chipping, flaking, or repainting needed.

Fully Breathable

Allows moisture vapor to escape from inside your home, preventing damp and condensation.

10-Year Guarantee

We stand by our work. Our Silicone Rendering applications come with a comprehensive 10-year warranty.

Professional application for lasting results:

  1. Masking & Protection – Protecting windows and doors
