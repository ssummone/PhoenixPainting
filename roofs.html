Roof Refurbishment Glasgow

Restore, Protect, & Enhance Your Roof

A damaged or moss-covered roof isn't just unsightly—it can lead to serious structural issues. Our comprehensive roof refurbishment service cleans, repairs, and coats your roof to look brand new and last for decades.

Complete Care for Your Roof

Deep Cleaning & Moss Removal

We safely remove moss, algae, and lichens that damage tiles and block gutters, restoring your roof's original appearance.

Tile Repairs & Pointing

We replace broken tiles and repoint ridges, verges, and valleys to ensure your roof is completely watertight.

Protective Coatings

Our advanced roof coatings add a coloured, waterproof barrier that extends the life of your tiles and insulates your home.

10-Year Guarantee

Our roof refurbishment services come with a 10-year warranty, giving you total peace of mind.

Advanced Coatings

We use specialist roof coatings designed for Scottish weather:

  • Super Hydrophobic – Repels water and prevents moss regrowth
  • Breathable – Prevents damp and condensation
  • Thermal Efficiency – Helps retain heat in your home
  • Multiple Colours – Restore or change your roof colour

Our Process

A thorough restoration system:

  1. Power Wash – Deep cleaning to remove all debris
  2. Fungicidal Wash – Killing remaining spores
  3. Repair – Replacing damaged tiles and mortar
  4. Coating – Two coats of premium sealant/paint
